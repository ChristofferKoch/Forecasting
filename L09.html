v. General Notation

The Box-Jenkins ARMA(p,q) model is denotedby the equation

\[ y_t = \phi_0 + \phi_1 y_{t-1} + \phi_1 y_{t-1} + \cdots + + \theta_1 a_{t-1} + \theta_1 a_{t-1} + \theta_1 a_{t-1} \]

The white noise error terms \(a_t\) are assumed to have the following properties:

  1. \(\mathbb{E}(a_t)=0, \quad \forall t\) (zero mean assumption)
  2. \(\mathbb{E}(a_t^2)=\sigma_a^2, \quad \forall t\) (constant variance assumption)
  3. \(\mathbb{E}(a_s a_t), \quad \forall t\neq s\) (independence of errors assumption)
  4. \(a_t\) are normally distributed
